MAUREEN

A Scream Origin Story by Colton Tran

Before Ghostface...
there was Maureen Prescott.

Explore the Film
Logline
For decades the Scream universe has been haunted by the murder that started it all. But we've never seen the woman behind the legend. This story explores the final chapter of Maureen Prescott's life — and the moment where tragedy quietly creates the Ghostface legacy.
Synopsis

In early-90s Hollywood, aspiring actress Maureen Prescott reinvents herself as Rina Reynolds, chasing fame in a world built on illusion. But after witnessing a brutal secret inside producer John Milton's powerful inner circle, she's pulled into a nightmare where silence is survival — and everyone is watching.

As her career rises, so does the danger around her. Someone is studying her, calling her, following her… rewriting her life into something darker.

Because in this town, stories don't just get told...
they get twisted.

And someone has already decided how hers ends.

Characters
Maureen Prescott / "Rina Reynolds" Late 20s – Early 30s
Emma Roberts

An ambitious and magnetic woman determined to outrun her past. In Hollywood, she reinvents herself as Rina Reynolds, chasing fame with sharp instincts and quiet resilience. But when she becomes entangled in a deadly secret, Maureen is forced to navigate a world where success demands silence — and survival comes at a cost.

As the pressure closes in, she fights to reclaim her identity… before it's erased completely.

Roman Bridger Late 20s
Aspiring Filmmaker

A quiet, observant aspiring filmmaker with an unsettling intensity. Roman has spent his life searching for the mother who abandoned him — and when he finds her living under a new identity, his fascination becomes obsession.

He watches. Records. Studies.

But when Maureen rejects him, his need to be seen turns into something far more dangerous — transforming him from observer… into author of her fate.

John Milton 40s – 50s
Hollywood Producer

A powerful Hollywood horror producer who controls the industry from the shadows. Charismatic, calculated, and untouchable, Milton operates in a world where ambition and exploitation are inseparable.

To him, fear is a tool — and people are expendable.

When Maureen witnesses the truth behind his circle, he offers her a choice: stay silent and rise… or speak out and disappear.

Hank Loomis Late 30s – 40s
Maureen's Anchor

Charming, grounded, and seemingly safe — Hank becomes a source of comfort for Maureen in the chaos of Hollywood. Their connection feels real, but it comes with consequences neither of them fully understand.

His presence represents the life Maureen could have had — and the one that will ultimately be destroyed.

Gale Weathers Early 20s
Aspiring Reporter

A sharp, relentless aspiring reporter hungry for her first big story. Gale briefly crosses paths with Maureen while investigating rumors surrounding Hollywood's darker undercurrents.

Driven and observant, she sees what others ignore — and understands that behind every headline… there's something worse.

Sidney Prescott Age 8 – 10
Voice / Child

Maureen's daughter back home in Woodsboro. Heard only through a phone call, Sidney represents the life Maureen is trying to return to — and the innocence that will one day be shattered by the events set in motion here.

The Actress Early 20s
Opening Victim

A hopeful young performer chasing her big break in Hollywood. Eager, vulnerable, and full of promise, she becomes the first glimpse into the true cost of ambition in a system that feeds on desperation.

Her death sets the tone:
In this world, dreams don't just die —
they're taken.
